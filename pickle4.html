Oh Snap!

Oh Snap! pickles are infamous for their pouch packaging. This makes the pickles perfect for an on the go snack! They also dont add the brine to the pouches so that opening them isnt a mess. Despite being stored in a bag, these pickles are very crunchy and delicious and have a great flavor!
